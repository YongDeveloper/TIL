# HTML

- HTML 코딩을 위한 연습2

``` html
<html>
  <body style="text-align:center";>
 
  <p> mission is to stop the <strong> degradation </strong> our planet ... <!-- 굵게 -->
  
  <p> Tokyo is the capital of Japan, the most populous <em> metropolitan </em> area in the world. <!-- 밑줄 -->
  
  <p> HTML is <mark> fun </mark> to learn <!-- 형광펜 -->
  
  <p> H<sub>2</sub>O is the scientific term for water. </p> <!-- 아래첨자-->
  
  <p> My favorite color is <del> blue</del> red. <!-- 취소줄긋기 -->
  
  <p> I am so <q> cool </q>. <!-- 인용구(쌍따옴표) -->
  
  
  </body>
</html>



```
